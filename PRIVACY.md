# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 6, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Simon Says - Simply Speak" assistive tool hosted at [https://github.com/juan-rey/SimonSays](https://github.com/juan-rey/SimonSays). 

We are committed to providing a secure and private experience for users who rely on our assistive technology. Our privacy philosophy is simple: **We do not collect, store, track, or sell your data.**

### 1. Data Collection & Accessibility Privacy
The Simon Says assistive tool operates entirely locally on your device. 
* **No Personal Data:** We do not collect names, emails, or contact information.
* **No Input Tracking:** The application does not log, monitor, or transmit any user inputs, keystrokes, gestures, or assistive device interactions.
* **No Telemetry:** There are no background analytics, crash reporting tools, or usage tracking scripts embedded in the software.

### 2. Data Storage
Because the application does not collect any information, no data is ever transmitted to or stored on external servers, cloud databases, or third-party infrastructure. 

### 3. Data Sharing and Selling
We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data. Because we have no access to your data, it is impossible for us to monetize or distribute it to third parties or advertisers.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to ensure it remains accurate to the tool's development. Any updates will be posted directly to this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions or feedback regarding privacy or accessibility features, please feel free to open an issue directly in the GitHub repository.
